Choose horizontal or vertical CO2 profile

For a selected area of interest you can either generate;
  • Horizontal Profile - The user assigns a target depth and area of interest. For the target depth using the geothermal model the program calculates the CO2 state/density at a 0.01°x0.01° resolution (fig. A).
  • Vertical Profile - The user selects a range of depths of interest (e.g. 500m to 1500m). For the selected area the model calculates the subsurface temperature and CO2 state/density throughout the depth range (fig. B).
