2.1.4 MODIS satellite land surface temperature data (Bechtel, 2015)

In order to accurately determine the temperature at-formation-depth the land surface temperature is of significant importance. We use the mean annual land surface temperature as observed by the MODIS satellite (Bechtel, 2015) to constrain this value. This data has been lightly reprocessed to a 0.1x0.1° Lat/Lon resolution to be used in this study (fig. 7).
