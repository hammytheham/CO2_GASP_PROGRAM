Abreviations

The following are abreviations to describe the three different datasets that underpin this study.
  • PWGD - the USGS Produced Water database (Blondes et al., 2016)
  • SMUH - geothermal gradient data derived from the Southern Methodist University Heatflow database (Blackwell et al., 2011)
  • MODIS - MODIS satellite derived land surface temperatures (Bechtel, 2015)
